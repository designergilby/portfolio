NANOLEAF CAMPAIGN

ad campaign
fall 2022
student project

The Nanoleaf Color Campaign was a project completed for an advertising class in the Fall of 2022. It lasted one semester and tasked the class to create a comprehensive campaign for a company of our choice. The company used was Nanoleaf, a modular smart lighting company that caters to a millennial gaming crowd. The idea to cast a wider net with this project, focusing on the benefits of ambient lighting, from improving mood to enhancing activities. We were required to create collateral for print, outdoor, interactive, and commercial advertisements.
The approach was to create a contemporary visual style focused on bold coloring that caters to a wider Millennial and Generation Z audience. The print ads were created to create a baseline for the styling, with the outdoor advertisements to simplify and solidify the visual language. The interactive ad concepts were created with Adobe After Effects and Blender to allow users to interact with the product on a more intimate level.
