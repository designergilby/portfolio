student project

This poster series was done for a Graphic Design class during the Fall 2021 semester, and the task was to create a poster based on new technology to gain more exposure. The project was completed over the span of one month. The chosen topic for this project was AR technologies.
The idea was to convey the benefits of utilizing AR technologies in professional settings, with the visuals implying tasks that can be achieved with augmented reality. The style uses a muted color palette with a bitmapped collage to combine natural and technology aesthetics.
