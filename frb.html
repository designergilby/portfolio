The design internship at the Federal Reserve Bank of Minneapolis was attended during the Summer of 2022 and lasted from early June to late August. It was a hybrid experience working collaboratively with the rest of the design team on both on-site designs as well as social media assets. It contained logo design, motion design, graphic design, photography, and signage design.
It tasked me with creating designs on a slow and fast timeline, and had me work with the branding guidelines of the Federal Reserve Bank. It also provided experience in presenting design works to potential clients. Shown below are two examples of public social media posts. A majority of the projects are not available to be posted publicly, but can be provided upon request.
